Our Story

"Our organisation was envisaged during the post-graduation years of our founder, Mr. Praveen Kumar when he visited the district prison of Kalyan, Maharashtra as a part of his field visit while pursuing his MA in Social Work, Criminology and Justice from TISS, Mumbai. He conducted a preliminary research and comprehended the importance of working within Criminal Justice Systems in his home State of Bihar. He was awarded a Criminal Justice Fellowship, TISS when he shared his idea with his mentor Prof. Vijay Raghavan. He initiated the work in 2016 and consequently received permission from the Chief Justice of Patna High Court Justice Iqbal Ahmed Ansari, to work inside the prison. Further, he collaborated with our co-founder Mr. Shubhendu Shekhar and Mr. Santosh Kumar to commence the organisation 'LAW Foundation".

Our Journey

  • 2016-2018

    Facilitated socio-legal aid services to UTPs in the Patna District as a Criminal Justice Fellow of TISS, Mumbai.

  • 2019

    Under the Indian Trust Act of 1882, LAW Foundation was registered as a trust.

  • 2020

    Constitutional compliances have been prepared.

    During the Covid-19 Lockdown, we provided dry ration kits to the families of around 150 prisoners.

    60 Inmates were released from Jail on P.R. Bail Bonds.

  • 2021

    During the second phase of COVID-19, dry ration kits were provided to about 300 families of Prisoners.

    Assisted victims of domestic violence with counselling and mental health support.

  • 2022

    Intervened in 212 cases. Finalist for the AGAMI Idea Award in 2022.

    Collaborated with several organizations. Initiated Sustainable Mental Health Program inside Prison.

  • 2023

    Developing Legal Literacy Programs in various Communities.

What We Do

Socio-Legal Aid Services

At LAW Foundation, we are dedicated to stepping up our efforts in addressing legal issue.We provide free socio legal aid services to the marginalised and vulnerable under-trial prisoners.

Socio-Legal Counselling

Our organisation provides quality legal advice for prisoners as well as their families to ensure speedy justice by way of securing bails, and assistance with respect to procedural legal intricacies, on a case to case basis.

Home Visit

Our organisation conducts home visits as a part of social aid outreach it help us to understand the specific needs of under-trials and their families. We link them with the appropriate reliefs available under various social protective schemes of the government.

Emergency Support Program

Our organisation provides dry ration kits to marginalised prisoners who are unable to secure their livelihoods and support families.

Rehabilitation and Social Reintegration

Our organisation rehabilitate and re-integrate stigmatised prisoners community within the society with the help of other organisation such as Centre of Equity Studies, HRLN, Aman Biradari, and KOSHISH.

Advocacy and Networking

Our organisation advocate its work at different platforms and networks with various social organisation.
