Who we are...

Mr. Praveen Kumar in the year 2016 came to Bihar under a Criminal Justice Fellowship Program of Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai. In the course of fellowship, he intervened in several prisons and issues of socio-legal aid. After a sustained endeavor of three years of extending socio-legal aid services towards custodial population lodged in various jails of Bihar. He met Santosh Kumar and Shubhendu Shekhar during his fellowship journey. They all encountered several issues pertaining towards disadvantaged custodial population. Thus they registered LAW Foundation as a Trust in 2019 whose core work revolves around the areas of socio-legal aid services, networking, training, research, and documentation, besides advocacy of custodial, institutional, legal, and constitutional rights of the custodial population. It is also focused on the rehabilitation and re-integration of socioeconomically marginalized and vulnerable individuals and groups.

Advocacy and networking

Socio-Legal Aid / Prison Visit

At LAW Foundation, we are dedicated to stepping up our efforts in addressing legal issue.We provide free socio legal aid services to the marginalised and vulnerable under-trial prisoners.

Legal Counselling

Our organisation provides quality legal advice for prisoners as well as their families to ensure speedy justice by way of securing bails, and assistance with respect to procedural legal intricacies, on a case to case basis.

Home Visit

Our organisation conducts home visits as a part of social aid outreach it help us to understand the specific needs of under-trials and their families. We link them with the appropriate reliefs available under various social protective schemes of the government.

Emergency Support Program

Our organisation provides dry ration kits to marginalised prisoners who are unable to secure their livelihoods and support families.

Rehabilitation and Social Reintegration

Our organisation rehabilitate and re-integrate stigmatised prisoners community within the society with the help of other organisation such as Centre of Equity Studies, HRLN, Aman Biradari, and KOSHISH.

Advocacy and Networking

Our organisation advocate its work at different platforms and networks with various social organisation.
