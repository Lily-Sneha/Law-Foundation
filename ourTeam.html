Who we are...

Mr. Praveen Kumar in the year 2016 came to Bihar under a Criminal Justice Fellowship Program of Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai. In the course of fellowship, he intervened in several prisons and issues of socio-legal aid. After a sustained endeavor of three years of extending socio-legal aid services towards custodial population lodged in various jails of Bihar. He met Santosh Kumar and Shubhendu Shekhar during his fellowship journey. They all encountered several issues pertaining towards disadvantaged custodial population. Thus they registered LAW Foundation as a Trust in 2019 whose core work revolves around the areas of socio-legal aid services, networking, training, research, and documentation, besides advocacy of custodial, institutional, legal, and constitutional rights of the custodial population. It is also focused on the rehabilitation and re-integration of socioeconomically marginalized and vulnerable individuals and groups.

Praveen Kumar
Praveen Kumar is the Director of the organization and leads strategy, project implementation, and fundraising. Praveen is a Criminal Justice Fellow (2016-19) at Centre for Criminology & Justice TISS, Mumbai. In 2016 he started working on the issues of under-trial prisoners and provided them free socio-legal aid services in the State of Bihar under the supervision of Prof. Vijay Raghavan. In 2019, he was awarded the Praxis Fellowship for his grassroot level intervention in the Criminal Justice System. He has been working continuously on the issues of marginalized communities who are imprisoned and are unable to secure due Justice. He has several publications in his name on Caste, Criminal Justice System, and the issues of Under-trial Prisoners in Economics & Political Weekly, Taylor & Francis (Routledge), SAGE, and editorial pieces.
Shubhendu Kumar
Shubhendu Shekhar is the CEO of the organization and leads the research, project operation, team members and communication. He has done his B.A., LL.B from National Law University, Jodhpur, and has been a part of this organization since its inception. He had been awarded Clinton Global Initiative University Fellowship 2021 and the Oxford Royal Academy Scholarship 2021. He has been working continuously on the issues of marginalized communities who are imprisoned and are unable to secure due Justice. He has several publications in his name on Caste, Criminal Justice System, and issues of Under-trial Prisoners in Economics & Political Weekly, Taylor & Francis (Routledge), SAGE, and editorial pieces.
Santosh Kumar
Santosh Kumar is the Senior Counsel of the organisation. He is continuously working with our organization on the socio-legal issues of marginalized communities, Notified and De-Notified Tribes. He has provided free socio-legal aid services to more than five hundred vulnerable inmates in the State of Bihar. He completed his LL.B from Patna University and also holds a degree on Rural Development.
