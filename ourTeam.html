Who we are

Mr. Praveen Kumar in the year 2016 came to Bihar under a Criminal Justice Fellowship Program of Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai. In the course of fellowship, he intervened in several prisons and issues of socio-legal aid. After a sustained endeavor of three years of extending socio-legal aid services towards custodial population lodged in various jails of Bihar. He met Santosh Kumar and Shubhendu Shekhar during his fellowship journey. They all encountered several issues pertaining towards disadvantaged custodial population. Thus they registered LAW Foundation as a Trust in 2019 whose core work revolves around the areas of socio-legal aid services, networking, training, research, and documentation, besides advocacy of custodial, institutional, legal, and constitutional rights of the custodial population. It is also focused on the rehabilitation and re-integration of socioeconomically marginalized and vulnerable individuals and groups.

Mr. Praveen Kumar
Director & Founder
Praveen Kumar is the Director of the organization who oversees fundraising, project delivery, and strategy for the Organization. Praveen is a Criminal Justice Fellow at TISS Centre for Criminology & Justice (2016–19). Under the direction of Prof. Vijay Raghavan, he began focusing on the problems affecting inmates who were awaiting trial in 2016 and offering them free socio-legal assistance services in the State of Bihar. For his grassroots involvement in the criminal justice system, he received the Praxis Fellowship in 2019. He has been working nonstop on issues of underprivileged groups that are incarcerated and unable to receive fair justice. He has multiple works published under his name in the Economics & Political Weekly, Taylor & Francis (Routledge), SAGE, and other journals on caste, the criminal justice system, and the problems with under-trial prisoners.
Shubhendu Kumar
Co-Founder
Shubhendu Shekhar is the Co-Founder of the organization and leads the research, project operation, team members and communication. He has done his B.A., LL. B from National Law University, Jodhpur, and has been a part of this organization since its inception. He currently completed his LLM from TISS, Mumbai in Access to Justice. He had been awarded Clinton Global Initiative University Fellowship 2021 and the Oxford Royal Academy Scholarship 2021. He has been working continuously on the issues of marginalized communities who are imprisoned and are unable to secure due Justice. He has several publications in his name on Caste, Criminal Justice System, and issues of Under-trial Prisoners in Economics & Political Weekly, Taylor & Francis (Routledge), SAGE, and editorial pieces.
Adv. Santosh Kumar
Co-Founder & Senior Counsel
Santosh Kumar is the Co-Founder cum Senior Counsel of the organization. He is continuously working with our organization on the socio-legal issues of Disadvantaged communities, Notified and De-Notified Tribes. He has provided free socio-legal aid services to more than five hundred vulnerable inmates in the State of Bihar. He completed his LL. B from Patna University and also holds a Master degree on Rural Development.
Shalini
Law Officer
Shalini is an advocate with an LL.M. in I.P.R. She is passionate about creating impact by engaging with people in the communities, empowering them, and educating them about their rights and duties. She handles the administrative work of the organization along with her duties of a LAW Officer. Apart from this she is leading the socio-legal aid project of the organization. From her engagement with this organization since 2021 she has appeared in near about 80 cases and secured bail in nearly 60 cases with an acquittal in one case.
Aniket Shekhar
Accountant
Aniket Shekhar manages the account section of LAW Foundation facilitating core responsibilities of the accounts i.e., Monitoring accounts, tracking financial records, Maintenance of proper books of accounts, Processing invoices, etc., and ensuring smooth handling of account-related operations of the projects allocated to the organization. A graduate from Lovely Professional University, Punjab in BBA finance; he has a working experience of 3 years in account-related matters of various non-governmental organizations.
Shahnaz Firdous
Social Worker
Shahnaz is engaged with the organization as a social worker. She has completed M.A. in social work from Patna University, Bihar. Since the completion of her Master’s Program, she was engaged with various Non-Governmental Organizations. She holds abundant of experience in working with young urban women on issues of personal hygiene. In addition, she also efficient in conducting regular community visits, resource mobilization, etc.
Gurudev Nanda
Social Worker
Gurudev, a TISS Guwahati alumnus, has a master's degree in social anthropology. He engaged himself with the organization in 2022. As a passionate, active young person, his perspective brings a unique value to the organization's work. He has now visited a number of Bihar jails and, using his great interpersonal skills, has counselled a number of prisoners. Gurudev also pays a visit to the prisoner's family at their residence and offers them any assistance they require including emergency support. He also works on the Sustainable Mental Health Project within the prison, which aims to protect the inmates from mental illness there.
Md. Irfan Alam
PAHEL Fellow
Md. Irfan is a lawyer and a first-generation learner. In July 2022, he graduated from Teerthanker Mahaveer University with a B.A.LL.B. (Hons.). Irfan has assisted in many criminal cases and provided socio-legal aid to many prisoners and their families. With his excellent communication skills, he has also promoted and offered legal literacy in various disadvantaged communities.
Seema Kumari
Community Mobilizer
Seema Kumari being the youngest member in the organization, is very enthusiast towards understanding the needs of community. She is currently pursuing her B.A. (Hons.) in Hindi from Patliputra University in Bihar. She has located several villages, engaged in communication with them, and provided assistance towards mobilizing the socio-legal aid needs of the communities.
Sanjeev Kumar
Advocate
Sanjeev Kumar an advocate with M.A. in Sociology from Jawaharlal Nehru University and LLB from Delhi University. He is associated with LAW Foundation from the last three years. He handles legal aid cases of the organization in the Jehanabad district. He envisions an egalitarian society where there is equality of opportunity, meaningful livelihood, dignity and compassion. He puts all his efforts into making the desired changes.
