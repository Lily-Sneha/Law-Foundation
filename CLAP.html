CLAP: Children Legal Aid & Protection

Children Legal Aid & Protection is an initiative taken by our organisation which focuses upon Children in Conflict with law who have alleged to commit acts against law and towards child who needs care and protection. The program will endeavor towards facilitating access to socio-legal aid, fundamental right to education, reintegration, and rehabilitation of such children in the society.

Children in Conflict with Law

An exploratory study was conducted in collaboration with UNICEF & CNLU Patna, where CICL remanded in prisons as adults was identified in Bihar. This study showed that several children who were below 18 years were sent to jail by portraying them as adults.
